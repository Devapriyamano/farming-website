About Aadhavan Iyarkai Thottam

Welcome to ஆதவன் இயற்கை தோட்டம், where nature thrives and pure, organic farming comes to life. We are a family-owned farm dedicated to cultivating a variety of organic produce, using sustainable practices that honor the land and nourish our community. Our farm specializes in traditional varieties, grown with utmost care, without synthetic chemicals or fertilizers.

At Aadhavan Iyarkai Thottam, our mission is to revive the ancient wisdom of organic farming, providing healthy, chemical-free produce that not only benefits the environment but also supports the well-being of our consumers. Each season, we carefully nurture our crops from seed to harvest, following natural rhythms and techniques passed down through generations.

In addition to our crops, our farm is also home to a delightful family of animals who are integral to our farm's ecosystem. We have two cows and a baby calf, who help enrich the soil naturally, a friendly cat with her two kittens, and two loyal dogs who keep watch over our fields. Together, they contribute to the life and vitality of our farm.

From seasonal fruits like bananas and papayas to our prized traditional rice varieties such as Seeragasamba, Thooyamalli, and Mappilaisamba, we are proud to offer a selection that celebrates Tamil Nadu’s rich agricultural heritage. Join us on our journey towards a healthier, greener future by embracing fresh, authentic, and sustainably grown produce straight from our fields.
